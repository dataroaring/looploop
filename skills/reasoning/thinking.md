When facing a complex problem, use structured thinking:

## Approach
1. **Decompose** — Break the problem into smaller, manageable parts
2. **Analyze** — Examine each part independently
3. **Synthesize** — Combine findings into a coherent solution
4. **Verify** — Check your reasoning for logical gaps

## When to Use
- Multi-step problems where order matters
- Problems with dependencies between parts
- Situations where the answer isn't immediately obvious

## Output Format
Think through each step explicitly before giving your final answer. Show your work when it helps the user understand your reasoning.
